🌟 Department of Tamil 🌟
Welcome to the Department of Tamil

🎓 The Department of Tamil at GASC Sankarankovil is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and cultural activities. Our department celebrates the rich heritage of the Tamil language and literature, and fosters a vibrant academic environment.

🎯 Vision: To impart quality education in Tamil language and literature and to cultivate a deep appreciation for Tamil culture and values.

🚀 Mission: To nurture creative and critical thinking, promote research, and encourage participation in literary and cultural events.

📚

Rich Curriculum

Our comprehensive curriculum covers classical, medieval, and modern Tamil literature, grammar, and linguistics with innovative teaching methods.

🎉

Cultural Programs

We organize vibrant seminars, workshops, and literary festivals to celebrate Tamil heritage and promote cultural awareness.

👨‍🏫

Experienced Faculty

Our distinguished faculty members are dedicated scholars and passionate educators committed to student success.
