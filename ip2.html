Government Arts and Science College - Sankarankovil

Government Arts and Science College (Co-Ed), Sankarankovil is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality higher education in the arts and sciences. Located in the vibrant town of Sankarankovil in Tenkasi District, our college has been serving students with excellence and commitment.

Academic Departments

Our college offers diverse academic programs across various disciplines

Department of Tamil

Tamil language studies explore one of the world's oldest classical languages covering ancient literature and modern Tamil writing. Students develop expertise in literary analysis, translation, and cultural interpretation.

Department of English

Provides comprehensive English language and literature programs with emphasis on communication skills, literary analysis, and critical thinking.

Department of Sociology

Studies human society, social behavior, and cultural patterns through research methodology, social theory, and fieldwork analysis.

Department of Commerce

Offers business education covering accounting, finance, marketing, taxation, and management principles for corporate and entrepreneurial careers.

Department of Computer Science

Provides education in programming, software development, algorithms, and emerging technologies including AI and cybersecurity.

Department of Statistics

Focuses on data analysis, probability theory, statistical modeling, and research methodology using modern statistical software and techniques.
